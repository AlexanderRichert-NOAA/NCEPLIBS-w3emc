SUBPROGRAM: W3FP12 CREATES THE PRODUCT DEFINITION SECTION PRGMMR: MCCLEES ORG: NMC421 DATE:92-01-14.

ABSTRACT: FORMATS THE PRODUCT DEFINITION SECTION ACCORDING TO THE SPECIFICATIONS SET BY WMO. USING O.N. 84 ID'S (1ST 8 WORDS) AS THE INPUT DATA. NEW SUBROUTINE CORRESPONDS TO THE REVISION #1 OF THE WMO GRIB STANDARDS MADE MARCH 15, 1991.

PROGRAM HISTORY LOG: 91-07-30 MCCLEES,A.J. NEW SUBROUTINE WHICH FORMATS THE PDS SECTION FROM THE O.N. 84 ID'S FROM THE GRIB EDITION 1 DATED MARCH 15, 1991.

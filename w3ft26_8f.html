SUBPROGRAM: W3FT26 CREATES WAFS 1.25X1.25 THINNED GRIDS PRGMMR: FARLEY ORG: W/NMC42 DATE: 93-04-28.

ABSTRACT: CONVERTS A 360X181 1-DEGREE GRID INTO A NH OR SH 360X91 1-DEGREE GRID. THIS NH/SH GRID IS FLIPPED FOR GRIB PURPOSES AND THEN CONVERTED TO THE DESIRED 1.25 DEGREE WAFS (QUADRANT) THINNED GRID.

PROGRAM HISTORY LOG: 93-04-28 FARLEY ORIGINAL AUTHOR 94-04-01 R.E.JONES CORRECTIONS FOR 1 DEG. DISPLACEMENT OF GRIDS AND ERROR IN FLIPPING OF SOUTHERN HEMISPHERE. 94-05-05 R.E.JONES REPLACE SUBR. W3FT01 WITH W3FT16 AND W3FT17. 94-06-04 R.E.JONES CHANGE SUBROUTINE NAME FROM WFSTRP TO W3FT26

USAGE: CALL W3FT26 (MAPNUM,FLD,HI,IGPTS,NSTOP) INPUT ARGUMENT LIST: MAPNUM - NUMBER OF GRID, 37 TO 44 FLD - NORTHERN OR SOUTHERN HEM. SPECTRAL FIELD

OUTPUT ARGUMENT LIST: HI - INTERPOLATED WAFS FIELD (3447 POINTS) IGPTS - NUMBER OF POINTS IN INTERPOLATED FIELD NSTOP - 24, WHEN MAPNUM .NE. 37 THRU 44

LIBRARY:
W3LIB - W3FT16, W3FT17

ATTRIBUTES: LANGUAGE: CRAY CFT77 FORTRAN MACHINE: CRAY C916-128, CRAY Y-MP8/864, CRAY Y-MP EL2/256

3 C> SUBPROGRAM: W3FB00 LATITUDE, LONGITUDE TO I,J
4 C> AUTHOR: HEERMANN,A. ORG: W345 DATE: 69-08-01
5 C>
6 C> ABSTRACT: CONVERTS THE COORDINATES OF A LOCATION ON EARTH FROM THE
7 C> NATURAL COORDINATE SYSTEM OF LATITUDE/LONGITUDE TO THE GRID (I,J)
8 C> COORDINATE SYSTEM OVERLAID ON THE POLAR STEREOGRAPHIC MAP PRO-
9 C> JECTION TRUE AT 60 N. A PREFERABLE, MORE FLEXIBLE SUBROUTINE TO
10 C> USE IS W3FB04. W3FB00 IS THE REVERSE OF W3FB01.
11 C>
12 C> PROGRAM HISTORY LOG:
13 C> 69-08-01 A. HEERMANN
14 C> 90-08-31 R.E.JONES CONVERT TO CRAY CFT77 FORTRAN
15 C>
16 C> USAGE: CALL W3FB00 (ALAT, ALONG, XMESHL, XI, XJ)
17 C>
18 C> INPUT VARIABLES:
19 C> NAMES INTERFACE DESCRIPTION OF VARIABLES AND TYPES
20 C> ------ --------- -----------------------------------------------
21 C> ALAT ARG LIST LATITUDE IN DEG. (-20.0(S. HEMIS) ) ALAT ) 90.0)
22 C> ALONG WEST LONGITUDE IN DEGREES
23 C> XMESHL ARG LIST MESH LENGTH OF GRID IN KILOMETERS AT 60N
24 C>
25 C> OUTPUT VARIABLES:
26 C> NAMES INTERFACE DESCRIPTION OF VARIABLES AND TYPES
27 C> ------ --------- -----------------------------------------------
28 C> XI ARG LIST I OF THE POINT RELATIVE TO NORTH POLE
29 C> XJ ARG LIST J OF THE POINT RELATIVE TO NORTH POLE
30 C>
31 C> SUBPROGRAMS CALLED:
32 C> NAMES LIBRARY
33 C> ------------------------------------------------------- --------
34 C> COS SIN SYSLIB
35 C>
36 C> REMARKS: THE GRID USED IN THIS SUBROUTINE HAS ITS ORIGIN (I=0,J=0)
37 C> AT THE NORTH POLE, SO IF THE USER'S GRID HAS ITS ORIGIN AT A
38 C> POINT OTHER THAN THE NORTH POLE, A TRANSLATION IS REQUIRED TO
39 C> GET I AND J. THE SUBROUTINE GRID IS ORIENTED SO THAT LONGITUDE
40 C> 80W IS PARALLEL TO THE GRIDLINES OF I=CONSTANT. THE RADIUS OF
41 C> THE EARTH IS TAKEN TO BE 6371.2 KM.
42 C> ALL PARAMETERS IN THE CALL STATEMENT MUST BE REAL
43 C> THIS CODE WILL NOT VECTORIZE ON A CRAY. YOU WILL HAVE PUT
44 C> IT LINE TO VECTORIZE IT.
45 C>
46 C> ATTRIBUTES:
47 C> LANGUAGE: IBM370 VS FORTRAN
48 C> MACHINE: CRAY Y-MP8/832
49 C>
50  SUBROUTINE w3fb00(ALAT,ALONG,XMESHL,XI,XJ)
51 C
52  DATA radpd /.01745329/
53  DATA earthr/6371.2/
54 C
55  re = (earthr * 1.86603) / xmeshl
56  xlat = alat * radpd
57  sinl = sin(xlat)
58  wlong = (along + 100.0) * radpd
59  r = (re * cos(xlat)) / (1. + sinl)
60  xi = r * sin(wlong)
61  xj = r * cos(wlong)
62  RETURN
63  END

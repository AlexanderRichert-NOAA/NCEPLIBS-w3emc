ONE-LINE GRIB TITLER FROM PDS SECTION.

Author
MCCLEES
Date
88-02-02

CONVERTS GRIB FORMATTED PRODUCT DEFINITION SECTION VERSION 1 TO A ONE LINE READABLE TITLE. GRIB SECTION 0 IS ALSO TESTED TO VERIFY THAT GRIB DATA IS BEING DECIPHERED.
